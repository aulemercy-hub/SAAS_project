Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Managing productivity shouldn’t be complicated. Here are answers to some of the most common questions about how FlowTask works and who it’s built for.

What is FlowTask and who is it for?

FlowTask is a productivity and task management platform designed for freelancers, remote teams, and startups who want a clearer, more focused way to plan and execute work.

Can I try FlowTask for free?

Yes. FlowTask offers a free plan so you can explore core features before upgrading to a paid plan.

Can I use FlowTask as a solo user?

Yes. FlowTask works perfectly for individuals managing personal or client tasks, and it scales easily when you’re ready to collaborate with a team.

Does FlowTask support team collaboration?

Absolutely. You can assign tasks, track progress, leave comments, and keep everyone aligned in real time—all from one dashboard.

How does the AI-powered planning work?

FlowTask helps break large goals into actionable steps and prioritizes tasks based on deadlines and urgency, making planning faster and more effective.

Is my data secure on FlowTask?

Yes. FlowTask is built using modern security best practices to ensure your data remains private, secure, and accessible only to you and your team.
